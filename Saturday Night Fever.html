Saturday Night Fever



Undecided on where to go to spend the evening with your company of friends?

Just for you, I have diligently dedicated myself to the arduous task of finding out which are the best places to host you and your cheerful crowd until late in the morning.

In San Myshuno there is really a place for all tastes.
Whether you are a non-stop karaoke party type or prefer to sip elaborate cocktails sitting on the top floor, overlooking the skyscrapers illuminated by day.

The Waterside Warble is a karaoke bar created from the renovation of a cannery.
Its rooms are very small, perfect for karaoke sessions for a few close friends, in which even the most out of tune of the company can feel at ease without fear of being heard by the whole neighbourhood in a free release.

Planet Honey Pop! it's a karaoke bar famous for its really great acoustics.
Located in the centre of the fashion district of San Myshuno, it is the right place if you want your karaoke group sessions to merge with the noise of the subway that flows beneath the venue and create unique harmonies.


Previous Next

The Stargaze Lounge is for the most romantic and influencers.
Located on the top floor of an ultra design penthouse, it is certainly the best place to enjoy the captivating panorama of the city that never sleeps.
