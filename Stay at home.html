“The ultimate luxury is being able to relax and enjoy your home”



At a time like this, where the authorities themselves are imposing it on us (more or less politely) even a simple concept as staying at home can be difficult to conceive.
But, if we stop to think about it ... staying at home is not that bad, isn't it?

When you are at home and find yourself with a lot of free time at your disposal, you can finally dedicate yourself to your passions and hobbies.
There are countless things to do!

But, if you feel confused by this whole situation, which, however you want to turn it, it always remains a little strange and surreal, it is comprehensible and here at Detoure we understand you perfectly.
I have prepared just for you a list of activities meant to fill these somewhat empty and sometimes boring days:


Reading a lot of books

Watching tons of movies or TV series

Preparing for that presentation that you will still have to do,
even if in live streaming

Doing Yoga in the living room

Cooking superfine meals

Dedicate yourself to spring cleaning with a new fervour

Playing videogames on the computer

Playing an instrument and get ready for a celebratory concert

Writing a book or a logbook of your days

Rediscovering your green thumb, even if you live in an apartment

Painting canvases on canvases like the New Botticelli and then resell them online

Previous Next

And these are just some of the ideas that came to my mind when writing this article, but the possibilities are endless.
We just need to be guided by inspiration and seize the moment!
