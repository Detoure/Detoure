The coolest venues of the moment



Evening dresses, flashes of photographers, crowds in raptures ... who has never dreamed the sparkling world of the star system with their eyes open?

The city of Del Sol Valley is precisely the place where "VIP" is the word capable of opening all doors.

But if you are not part of this world and you are not satisfied with just observing it from a distance, below I have prepared a list of the "hottest" places of the moment, where meeting a celebrity is on the agenda.

Orchid a Go Go
Not the lavishest of bars in Del Sol Valley but you can surely find some low to mid-level celebrities hanging out enjoying the grungiest atmosphere.


Previous Next

PBP Studio
It is the peak of the luxury club scene. Only the highest of celebrities will make it past the bouncer.
If you have a little extra cash you might be able to bribe the bouncer to sneak in.
Otherwise, you can be content to see the stars who come out of their limousines to enter the club and try to get an autograph before they cross the threshold.


Previous Next

Celebrity Boulevard
It is the place where all Del Sol Valley comes to see famous people and to show off.
Stroll along the main avenue to find out the names of the celebrities imprinted on the pavement and take a selfie with a star!
