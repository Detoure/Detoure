Detoure is a website that tells of adventures and experiences with the valid help of high-quality photographs.


For those who are wondering who is hiding behind the scenes... here I am!

Hi, I'm Isabelle, a girl born in the great metropolis of San Myshuno with the desire to know and discover new things in her blood.
I decided to open this website to tell the world about the travels that I do when I'm not at home studying books to get a degree in Fine Arts.

Since I was a child, I have always dreamed of travelling and exploring the world, but my family has always been more the type of family that prefers sedentary holidays in the usual place, every single summer, since time immemorial.

So, when I was old and independent enough to get by myself, I decided to move to a house of my own, where I could dedicate myself undisturbed to the realization of my dream.
After having done some odd jobs to raise some money, now as a babysitter now like an ice cream shop assistant, I decided to take the big step and leave, backpacking, towards new destinations.

I hope that what I share can be, for all of you who will discover this little corner of the Internet, a pleasant reading and maybe even an inspiration to start discovering all that the world has to offer!
