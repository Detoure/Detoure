How to have FUN at Sulani Islands


Hands up who has never wanted to spend their days lying on a sunbed on a tropical island?

The Sulani archipelago is what is called a true tropical paradise, with its white beaches of fine sand bathed by the waves of an incredible crystal clear sea.
The archipelago is made up of various islands, each with its unique characteristics:

Ohan'ali Town
It is the most inhabited island of Sulani, where lovers of sport and a good dose of fun can try their hand at numerous activities ranging from snorkelling to motor surfing. Full of life and young people who are waiting for nothing more than an opportunity to celebrate and meet new people.
Do not miss a stop at The Sand Bar, the most popular place in Sulani, an emblem of how its inhabitants live a life that follows the rhythm of the waves breaking on the beach.


Lani St. Taz
A real corner of paradise, with idyllic landscapes to say the least.
White beaches and sky-coloured sea are the settings for an island famous for nature that reigns supreme. Here, if you have a little luck, you can get to know lovely dolphins and swim together in absolute tranquillity.
