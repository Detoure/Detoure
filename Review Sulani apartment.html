The house on the beach - Sulani Islands



When I was on vacation at the Sulani archipelago, I stayed in a lovely beach house.

A perfect apartment to host a family of about 3 or 4 people, featuring a colourful living room and a double bedroom with a peaceful and relaxed atmosphere.

The house is built with stilts that are set on the sand, thus providing a formidable view of the ocean.
It also has a spacious outdoor patio, ideal for sunbathing under the umbrella, without losing sight of the children playing happily in the water just beyond.
